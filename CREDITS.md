## How to Download from Freesound

1. Create a free account at freesound.org
2. Search for the sound
3. Filter by **CC0 (Public Domain)** license
4. Download as MP3
5. Optionally compress to 96kbps mono: `ffmpeg -i input.mp3 -b:a 96k -ac 1 output.mp3`
6. Place in `/public/audio/` with the filename listed above
